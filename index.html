Vijayalaxmi Ashok Aralikatti

 

I am an undergraduate researcher at the Center of Excellence in Vision Intelligence(CEVI), KLE Technological University, specializing in computer vision and deep learning. My research journey spans both 2D and 3D domains, with significant experience in medical image analysis, particularly in oral cancer detection through histopathology segmentation. Building on my foundation in low-level vision systems and enhancing them, I am currently exploring dynamic human modeling in 3D space, leveraging tools like Blender and MeshLab. I am deeply passionate about advancing the field of computer vision and eager to contribute to cutting-edge research that bridges the gap between 2D and 3D vision applications. Additionally, I have experience in web development, along with a solid foundation in data structures and algorithms

I completed my Bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering from KLE Technological University, Hubballi. I majorly worked on Histopathology slides and Low-Level Vision Systems and published in the area, advised by Prof Uma Mudenagudi, and Ramesh Ashok Tabib.

         

 

Primary Research Areas

My primary research areas include Volumetric Capture Systems, 3D Human Modeling, Computer Vision Low-Level Vision And Enhancement, Histopathology slides analysis and annotation).
